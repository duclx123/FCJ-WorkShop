### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is professional, supportive, and highly conducive to learning. Team members maintain a friendly and approachable attitude, offering assistance whenever needed. The workspace is organized and comfortable, which contributes significantly to productivity. Nonetheless, additional team-building or social activities could further enhance internal engagement.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
My mentor provided clear, structured guidance and encouraged me to explore solutions independently before offering direct answers. The admin team offered timely support with documentation and administrative procedures, ensuring a smooth workflow. Their collective guidance played an essential role in my professional development during the internship.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The assigned tasks aligned closely with the knowledge I gained in university while also exposing me to new concepts relevant to industry practice. This balance allowed me to reinforce core academic foundations while expanding practical expertise.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
Throughout the internship, I gained valuable experience in project coordination tools, collaborative workflows, and professional communication. My mentor also shared practical insights from real industry scenarios, which helped me better understand potential career pathways and long-term development goals.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company promotes a positive and respectful culture. Team members work diligently while maintaining a welcoming atmosphere. During high-priority tasks, the team collaborates effectively regardless of position or seniority. This created a strong sense of inclusion and belonging for me as an intern.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The internship policies are fair and supportive, providing allowances and flexible working arrangements when necessary. Opportunities to participate in internal training sessions added significant value to the overall experience.
